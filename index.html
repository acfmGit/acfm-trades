Technical Documentation & FAQ

Get answers to common queries regarding indicators installation, execution rules, and setups logic.

Once your payment checkout completes and your invitation is approved, we add your TradingView username to the script's invite-only group. In your TradingView chart, go to Indicators -> Invite-only scripts -> Select "Advanced C2-C3 Fractal Model".

C2 CISD restricts the Change in State of Delivery validation to be confirmed within the bounds of the C2 candle. Regular CISD allows validation to extend into the next 3 HTF candles after C2. If a close crossing doesn't form within that extended range, the line freezes at the 3-candle limit and orderblock projections are skipped.

The model performs best with a 15:1 ratio: a 1-minute execution chart paired with 15-minute HTF candles, or a 5-minute execution chart paired with 1-hour HTF candles. Use the "Automatic" settings toggle to let the indicator pair them dynamically based on the current chart TF.
